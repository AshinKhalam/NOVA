UIDC (User Interface Designing Contest)

Are you good at designing but didn't get a chance to show it? Well ENCAMP 2.0 Phase 2 IEEE SB TKMCE, provides you with a chance to show your skills in a remarkable Online UI Designing Competition.

About The Event

Ever used Zomato or Swiggy and felt like the UI needs improvement? So here is the chance to give your idea about what it should have been. Design your own Food Delivery App.

We allow pen-paper designs too. So you don't have to worry about not having software skills.

And for the best part, it's free!

So what are you waiting for? Take up your designing skills and shine out bright among others.

Instructions

Submissions shall be unique and your own.

Participants should try out interesting techniques and show their personality in their work.

Only one submission per participant.

Participants are requested to submit their respective entries in provided google forms in pdf format. (maximum file size: 10MB)

Participants should make sure that the source file is shared with us.

Can be done using pen and paper or digitally using apps such as canva (refer to the video below)

Registration

Project shall be submitted by the 23rd of January 5:00pm.

Winners will be announced within a week.
